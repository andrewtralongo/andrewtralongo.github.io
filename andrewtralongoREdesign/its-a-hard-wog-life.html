It's a Hard Wog Life

It’s a Hard Wog Life is a zine about Italian grandparents. Inspired by the craziness of their behaviors and habits, such as keeping playing cards in the fridge and sewing up a new pair of ripped jeans thinking they are on the brink of being thrown away, it explores the playful and caring nature of these lovable characters.

“They never use the inside kitchen; It's the same concept as the 'good room' at the front of the house, which is only used for the most special of occasions, such as if the Pope came to visit. They can make as much as a mess as they like, spill anything anywhere while slowly collecting a pile of dishes by their side. All the pots and pans they use every day are covered in battle scars except for the Bessemer set which is touched with pride and love; a cooks best friend. But you can't forget to mention the knives which are so blunt that they practically rip a loaf of bread apart instead of cutting it.”
